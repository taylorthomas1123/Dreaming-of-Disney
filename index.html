Disney World Parks

Walt Disney World Resort is home to four unique theme parks and the popular Disney Springs entertainment district, each offering its own special experiences. From the fairy-tale magic of Magic Kingdom to the global cultures of EPCOT, the exciting adventures at Disney's Animal Kingdom, and the movie-inspired attractions at Disney's Hollywood Studios, there is something for every visitor. Disney Springs complements the parks with exceptional shopping, dining, and live entertainment, making it the perfect destination to relax after a day of adventure.
