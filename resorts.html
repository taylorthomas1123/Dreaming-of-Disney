Disney Resort hotels allow guests to stay in the heart of the magic while enjoying exclusive benefits, immersive Disney theming, and convenient transportation to the theme parks. With more than 25 resort hotels to choose from, there are accommodations to fit a variety of budgets and vacation styles. Disney resorts are grouped into four main categories: Value Resorts, Moderate Resorts, Deluxe Resorts, and Deluxe Villas. Value Resorts are the most affordable option and feature larger-than-life Disney themes, colorful décor, and family-friendly amenities. Moderate Resorts provide more spacious accommodations, enhanced dining options, and beautifully landscaped grounds. Deluxe Resorts feature premium accommodations located close to the theme parks, along with signature dining, themed pools, and additional recreational activities. Many Deluxe Resorts provide convenient access to the Monorail, Disney Skyliner, boats, or walking paths. Deluxe Villas are designed for families and larger groups who want extra space and home-like amenities. These accommodations often include kitchenettes or full kitchens, separate bedrooms, washers and dryers, and spacious living areas. Guests staying at Disney Resort hotels receive several exclusive benefits, including early entry to all four theme parks every day, complimentary transportation throughout Walt Disney World, the ability to purchase Lightning Lane passes through the My Disney Experience app, and early access to select dining reservations. Deluxe Resort guests also enjoy Extended Evening Theme Park Hours on select nights at certain parks. Whether you're looking for an affordable family vacation or a luxurious getaway, staying at a Disney Resort provides convenience, immersive theming, and easy access to the magic throughout your stay.
