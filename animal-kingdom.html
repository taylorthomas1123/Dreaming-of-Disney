Disney's Animal Kingdom combines thrilling attractions, live entertainment, and incredible wildlife experiences while celebrating the beauty of nature and animal conservation. The park is divided into several themed lands, each offering unique adventures for guests of all ages. Oasis is the park's entrance area, where lush walking trails introduce guests to beautiful gardens, waterfalls, and a variety of animals before reaching the main park. Discovery Island serves as the heart of Animal Kingdom and is home to the iconic Tree of Life withdetailed animal carvings. How many can you spot? Africa transports visitors to the fictional village of Harambe. The area's signature attraction, Kilimanjaro Safaris, takes guests on an open-air safari through habitats filled with giraffes, elephants, lions, rhinos, and many other African animals. Asia offers breathtaking scenery inspired by the Himalayan region. Guests can explore the Maharajah Jungle Trek to observe tigers, bats, and exotic birds. Pandora The World of Avatar immerses guests in the breathtaking world from the Avatar films. Coming Soon: Tropical Americas (Opening in 2027). Disney's Animal Kingdom is transforming the former DinoLand U.S.A. into an exciting new land inspired by the vibrant cultures, landscapes, and wildlife of Central and South America.
