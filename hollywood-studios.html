Disney's Hollywood Studios celebrates the magic of movies, television, and immersive storytelling by placing guests in the middle of their favorite films and adventures. The park is divided into several themed areas, each offering exciting attractions, live entertainment, dining, and unforgettable experiences. Hollywood Boulevard is the park's entrance and recreates the glamour of 1930s Hollywood. Lined with shops and restaurants, it leads guests to the iconic Chinese Theatre. Echo Lake features attractions inspired by classic adventure films. Guests can enjoy Indiana Jones Epic Stunt Spectacular!, a live-action show demonstrating movie stunt techniques. Toy Story Land shrinks guests to the size of a toy as they enter Andy's backyard. Star Wars: Galaxy's Edge transports visitors to the remote planet Batuu, where they can explore Black Spire Outpost, build a custom lightsaber or droid, and experience two of Disney's most immersive attractions: Millennium Falcon: Smugglers Run and Star Wars: Rise of the Resistance. Disney's Hollywood Studios offers a unique blend of thrilling attractions, immersive lands, spectacular live entertainment, and beloved Disney stories, making it a must-visit destination for movie lovers, Star Wars fans, and families alike.
