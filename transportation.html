Disney provides a complimentary transportation system that makes it easy for guests staying at Disney Resort hotels to travel throughout Walt Disney World without needing a personal vehicle. The transportation network includes buses, the Monorail, the Disney Skyliner, water transportation, and walking paths, allowing guests to conveniently travel between the theme parks, water parks, Disney Springs, and resort hotels. With multiple transportation options available, guests can easily explore the entire Walt Disney World Resort while enjoying the convenience and immersive experience that Disney transportation provides.

Transportation Areas Connected
Disney Buses All Disney Resort hotels, Magic Kingdom, EPCOT, Hollywood Studios, Animal Kingdom, and Disney Springs
Monorail Magic Kingdom, EPCOT, Disney's Contemporary Resort, Disney's Polynesian Village Resort, and Disney's Grand Floridian Resort
Disney Skyliner EPCOT, Hollywood Studios, Disney's Caribbean Beach Resort, Pop Century Resort, Art of Animation Resort, and Riviera Resort
Water Transportation Magic Kingdom area resorts, EPCOT, Hollywood Studios, Disney Springs, and select Disney Resort hotels
Walking Paths Nearby resorts to parks including Magic Kingdom, EPCOT, Hollywood Studios, and Disney Springs

Transportation Tips

  • Arrive early when traveling to the theme parks
  • Allow extra time during busy seasons
  • Use the My Disney Experience app for transportation updates
  • The Skyliner is a great option for EPCOT and Hollywood Studios
  • Check return transportation times before leaving a park
