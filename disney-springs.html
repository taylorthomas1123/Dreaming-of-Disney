Disney Springs is Walt Disney World's premier shopping, dining, and entertainment destination. Unlike the four theme parks, Disney Springs does not require admission, making it a popular place for both vacationers and local residents. The district is divided into four unique neighborhoods—Marketplace, Town Center, The Landing, and West Side—each offering its own selection of restaurants, shops, and entertainment. Marketplace is a family-friendly area featuring the world's largest Disney store, World of Disney, along with attractions like Marketplace Carousel, Marketplace Train Express, and unique shopping experiences such as The LEGO Store and Once Upon a Toy. Guests can also enjoy sweet treats from popular locations like Goofy's Candy Company and Ghirardelli Soda Fountain & Chocolate Shop. Disney Springs is also known for its outstanding dining options, with more than 60 restaurants, quick-service locations, snack stands, and dessert shops offering cuisines from around the world. Throughout the year, guests can enjoy seasonal events, live music, holiday celebrations, and special entertainment. Whether you're shopping for Disney souvenirs, enjoying a world-class meal, or simply taking a relaxing stroll along the waterfront, Disney Springs offers something for everyone and is the perfect way to end a day at Walt Disney World.
